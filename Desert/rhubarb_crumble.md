# Rhubarb Crumble

## Ingredients
### Topping
* 1 cup flour
* 1/3 cup oats
* 3/4 cup sugar
* pinch of salt
* 6 tablespoons melted butter
* 1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts

### Filling
* 2lb chopped rhubarb
* 1/3 cup sugar
* 1/4 cup flour
* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 1/2 teaspoon orange zest
* 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Preperation
1. Combine flour, oats, sugar and salt in a bowl
1. Stir in melted butter and hazelnuts
1. Squeeze into large crumbles and place in the freezer
1. Toss rhubarb, sugar, flour, vanilla extract, orange zest and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a 8-by-8-inch glass or ceramic baking dish
1. Scatter the crumble on top and bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven until golden and bubbly, 45 minutes
1. Let cool for 15 minutes; serve with whipped cream.
