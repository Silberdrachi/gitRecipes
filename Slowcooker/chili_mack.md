# Chili Mac

## Ingredients
* 1lb lean ground beef 
* 4 cloves garlic minced 
* 1 onion chopped 
* 28oz of canned tomato 
* 2 cups beef broth 
* 16oz of macaroni 
* 1 tbsp chili powder 
* 1/2 tsp cumin 
* 30oz chili beans

## Preperation
1. combined all in pot except noodles and cooked on low for 6 hours then added noodles and cooked for 30 more min
